20 Years Of Excellence

We are a registered body of 20 years old, founded in 2003. It is a very well-known and premier organization of Vishwakarma and represents the aims and aspirations of about Vishwakarma community living in Bengaluru and its suburbs. Vishwakarma Samaja Vedike is dedicated to the social, economic, educational and cultural enrichment of the people of Bengaluru.
